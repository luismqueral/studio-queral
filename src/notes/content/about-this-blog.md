this is not really a blog. it's more of a shared notebook — a place where I keep notes, recipes, experiments, half-baked ideas, and the occasional story that I think is worth telling.

some of these "posts" are polished. most aren't. many of them started as notes to myself that I decided to put online in case they're useful to someone else. think of it less like a publication and more like an open desk drawer.

### a few things to keep in mind

**nothing here is final.** I revise things constantly. posts get rewritten, reorganized, and sometimes quietly removed as my thinking changes. if you read something today, it might say something slightly different next month. that's by design — I'd rather keep things alive and evolving than freeze them in place.

**take everything with a grain of salt.** I'm not an authority on most of the things I write about. I'm a designer who likes to build things and think out loud. these are my notes from the process, not a textbook.

**the dates are approximate.** some of these pieces are from years ago and have been updated since. the date usually reflects when I first started writing something, not when it was last touched.

### what you'll find here

- notes on design, technology, and creative tools
- personal experiments and side projects
- recipes, music, and other non-work things
- old essays from college and early career that I still find interesting
- stuff that doesn't fit anywhere else

if something resonates or you want to talk about it, [send me an email](mailto:hey@queral.studio). I like hearing from people.
