# Hello World

This is the first post on the newly rebuilt site, now powered by [Astro](https://astro.build/).

## Why Astro?

Astro is perfect for content-heavy sites because it:

- Ships **zero JavaScript by default** (faster pages)
- Has built-in **Content Collections** for managing posts
- Lets you use **React components** where needed
- Generates **static HTML** for great SEO
